# Terminal Render Capture additions

This repository is a fork of SwiftTerm at upstream revision
`58915b1010d7dbc86d0e79dc2c40f0c183ccaf5b`.

The terminal render-capture observer, command-line tools, tests, and setup
article are maintained by Asparuh Koev under SwiftTerm's MIT license. Changes
are intentionally limited to draw-boundary observation and the tooling needed
to produce deterministic terminal-render evidence.
